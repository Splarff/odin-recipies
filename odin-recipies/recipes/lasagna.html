Lasagna

So, you got laid. Good shit. Lets clean that mouth of yours out...

Here's what you gotta do for the perfect lasagna dinner:

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce

Procedure

  1. Boil Pasta:Cook in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Prepare meat sauce:Cook meat with onion and garlic, drain, add sauce and simmer until thick.
  3. Combine cheese mixture:Stir the cheese mixture into a bowl.
  4. Layer and bake:Layer the sauce and cheese with lasagna noodles and bake until brown and bubbly.
